Battle Dragons
Game Synopsis Battle Dragons is my attempt at recreating the Intellivision classic Battle Tanks, with my own spin. Battle Dragons is a two player game where each player controls a dragon, either red or blue. After the players agree on a winning point total, they attempt to hit each other with fireballs gaining one point for each hit and ending the game at the designated score. But watch out! Your own fireballs can hurt you! Muahahha!!
